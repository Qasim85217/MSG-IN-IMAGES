WHITE KARAHI

IMAGES


INGREDINTS:

↑
  • 1 kg chicken (boneless)
  • 2 onions
  • 10,12 garlic buds
  • 8 green chillies
  • 1 inch ginger piece
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tblsp pepper corns
  • 1 tblsp ginger garlic paste
  • Half cup oil & 2 tblsp butter
  • 2 tsp salt or as your taste
  • 2 tblsp Coriander stems
  • 15,16 cashews
  • 10,12 almonds
  • 3,4 bay leaves
  • 2 black cardamom
  • 8 green cardamoms
  • 4 tblsp cream

METHOD:

↑
  1. Heat a pan n fry peppercorns for 2 mints, after cool down crushed them not make powder.
  2. Wash n cut the chicken in cubes add yogurt, 1,1 teasp salt, crushed black pepper, ginger garlic paste & marinate for 30 mints.
  3. Soak in Lukewarm water cashews n almonds
  4. Now take a Karahi or a deep pan add 2 tbl spoon oil,bay leaves,green & black cardamom, cloves n fry for 2 mints
  5. Now add all the veg ingredients in it n fry for more 2 mints,now add cashews n almonds fry for 1 mint more. then add half cup water,cover for 10 mints & let it soft.
  6. After cool down blend in a grinder & make a smooth paste.
  7. Take oil in a Karahi heat it & add butter now add the paste n cook for 3,4 mints.
  8. Now add marinated chicken cook on high flame for 2,3 mints then cover n let it cook until tender the chicken or dry the water. Now add more pepper & salt as you want.
